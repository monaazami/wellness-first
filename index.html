We endeavour to provide help and support for people who seek sanctuary in the UK.

Our goal is to empower refugees and asylum seekere to cope with their issues.

We are a young community group based in hartlepool. Our primary objectives consist of three elements First and foremost we focus on Refugeesand asylum seekers to cope with their health issues and refer them to relevant agencies if necessary . Secondly we try to guide refugee and asylum seekers through suitable training and help them to integrate in to society. Third we provide different activities to improve their wellbeing and they can meet other people and unwind themselves. health issues specialy mental health problems
